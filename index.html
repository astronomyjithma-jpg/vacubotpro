Core Capabilities

Built for intelligent
autonomous cleaning

Every system was engineered around one idea — a robot that understands your space, not just sweeps through it.

01

LiDAR 360° Mapping

Real-time spatial awareness with 12m range detection builds a precise floor plan of your home in under 3 minutes.

02

Adaptive Suction

3-stage suction system automatically adjusts from 800Pa to 2700Pa based on carpet vs. hardwood detection.

03

AI Room Recognition

On-device machine learning identifies room types and applies customized cleaning patterns without cloud processing.

04

Smart Scheduling

Learns your routine and proactively schedules cleanings when you're away — synced with your calendar and home sensors.

05

Auto-Return & Charge

Returns to base when battery drops to 15%, recharges, and resumes the exact path from where it left off.

06

Anti-Drop Cliff Sensors

Six infrared cliff sensors prevent falls from stairs, elevated platforms, and sudden floor changes with 99.8% accuracy.
